Why I got into Chess


I began playing chess around 5-6 years old when I played my grandfather for the very first time ever. He had scholars mated me a couple of times as I had no idea how to play at the time but that was where my love for chess had started.


He still to this day has refused to play me however my passion for chess has not waivered and I am still persuing my dreams in the chess world.
