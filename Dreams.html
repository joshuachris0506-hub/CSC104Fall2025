My Chess Career Goals


My chess goals are to become a titled player, specifically a FM rated player so that I may become a chess teacher and teach people all around the world chess. I never had any goal to be the best but I want people to experience chess and have fun with the game the same way I did growing up.


Getting to this goal will take time, but time I am willing to spend to achieve my dream job of being a chess teacher.
